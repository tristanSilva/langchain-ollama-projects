Naga City is a highly urbanized city in Camarines Sur, Philippines.
It is known as the Heart of Bicol. The city has a population of 
approximately 200,000 people. The local government is led by a 
city mayor and a city council. Major industries include education, 
healthcare, and retail. Naga City is home to the University of Nueva 
Caceres and Ateneo de Naga University. The Peñafrancia Festival is 
the most important religious and cultural event held every September.
The city is part of the Bicol Region, Region V of the Philippines.
Naga City was declared a highly urbanized city on December 8, 1948.
The Naga River runs through the city and is a significant landmark.
